About us

The Data Science Club of Amity University, Noida is built upon with a vision to create a mindful and proficient workforce. It acts as a platform for individuals who are passionate to build their career in the areas of data science and analytics. DSC ASET consists of a vast network of students and industry experts, who strive to ensure that every member gets a cutting edge advantage in implementing latest data science technologies and practical tools.
